About My Application

The core basis of my Application is to determine the most suitable
amount for the Client/Customer to borrow based on their financial
history including Salary and Credit Ratings, we may also check for
repayment history and any ongoing loans.

This application will take the Client/Customer given information
and check if they have met the banks borrowing requirements to
successfully determine the borrowing capacity if the Client/Customer
has met the requirements.
